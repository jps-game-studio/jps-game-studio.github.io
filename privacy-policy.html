Privacy Policy

Race Legends

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile game ("the Game") collects, uses, and protects information when you play. By installing or using the Game, you agree to the practices described in this document.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

2. Information We Collect

The Game collects only the minimum data required for gameplay, analytics, advertising, and in-app purchases.

2.1 Device Information (collected automatically)

We may collect basic technical information from your device, including:

  • device model and hardware information
  • operating system version
  • language and region settings
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • advertising identifier (GAID/AAID)
  • crash logs and performance data

This information is used solely to ensure the Game functions correctly and to improve stability.

2.2 Gameplay Data

We collect anonymous gameplay information such as:

  • progress and achievements
  • unlocked items
  • virtual currency balance
  • session length and frequency
  • interactions with menus and game features

This helps us improve game balance and user experience.

2.3 In-App Purchases (IAP)

When you make a purchase, we receive:

  • product ID
  • purchase status (success, canceled, refunded)
  • anonymous transaction identifiers

We do not receive or store any payment information. All payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing.

2.4 Advertising Data

If the Game displays ads, advertising partners (e.g., Google AdMob) may collect:

  • advertising identifier (GAID/AAID)
  • approximate location (country-level)
  • ad impressions and interactions
  • device type and OS version

This data is used to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

2.5 Analytics

We use anonymous analytics services (e.g., Unity Analytics, Firebase Analytics) to understand how players use the Game and to detect issues.

Collected data is aggregated and cannot identify individual users.

3. How We Use the Data

We use collected data to:

  • operate and improve the Game
  • fix bugs and optimize performance
  • process in-app purchases
  • show ads (if applicable)
  • prevent fraud and abuse
  • analyze gameplay trends

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Sharing of Data

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers such as:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob
  • Unity Services
  • Firebase Analytics

Each provider has its own privacy policy and complies with industry standards.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All communication with our services uses encrypted connections (HTTPS).

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • request deletion of your data
  • request information about stored data
  • opt out of personalized advertising (via device settings)

You can reset your advertising ID in your device settings at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available within the Game or on our website.
